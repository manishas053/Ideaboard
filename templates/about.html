About Page


This is the LabHub Idea Dashboard maintained by Network Lab Devops team. This tool helps you to submit your ideas or projects you think that helps in building Intel network better.

In the home page you can see the projects submitted by the team mates. You have the option to Like/Dislike the projects. On double clicking a post, you can see the details of the project and the comments. You can also post your comments.

You can submit your ideas by clicking "Submit Idea" on the right side of the navigation bar (you need to login to perform this action).




Once you click on submit idea, you will get a form which requires four fields as follows.

"Project Title" : This is the name of your idea which you are submitting

"Project Description" : Give a brief description about your idea

"Effort Required" : The effort/man hours required to finish the task

"Business Value" : The value that brings to the business by accomplishing the task

Once you are done with filling the required fields, you are ready to submit your idea.


After submitting you will be able to see your project in the homepage.



You can update/delete your post once it is created. For doing this double click on your post, you can see the options for this.


NOTE : You will not be able to delete your post, once review panel has started reviewing.

The review panel will review your ideas and give their feedback through comments. You can also see the count of approves/rejects for your idea. Once you project is approved you are ready to go !
